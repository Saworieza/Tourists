Sarova Shaba Package Itinerary

Day 1Nairobi- Samburu Sarova Shaba

Leave Nairobi for Samburu at 0700hrs to arrive in time for lunch. Check in your rooms and relax then leave for an evening game drive in the Shaba Game Reserve. Dinner and overnight stay at the lodge.

Day 2Sarova Shaba

After breakfast, leave for a day game drive at the Samburu game reserve home to the unique animals like Reticulated Giraffe and Gravyâ€™s zebras and return to the lodge in time for lunch. After lunch you may spend the rest of the afternoon relaxing by the poolside and enjoying yourself in the hotel . Dinner and overnight stay at the lodge.

Day 3Sarova Shaba - Nairobi

Start with an early morning game drive then return to the lodge for breakfast, enjoy part of the morning relaxing by the poolside or swimming on the last day. Check out of the hotel by 12.noon and embark on your journey to Nairobi and have lunch at a stopover of your choice to arrive in Nairobi in the evening.

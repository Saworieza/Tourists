AA Lodge Amboseli Package Itinerary

Day 1Nairobi - Amboseli

Leave Nairobi at 0700hrs for Amboseli and arrive in time for lunch. After lunch proceed to the park for afternoon game drive, spot large herds of elephants and plentiful plains game against the backdrop of Mount Kilimanjaro. Dinner and overnight at the lodge.

Day 2Amboseli

Take an early morning game drive to hunt for the big 5. Return to the lodge and enjoy the hotel facilities. At around four you will leave for an evening game drive or a guided nature walk. Dinner and overnight stay at the camp.

Day 3Amboseli - Nairobi

Start with an early morning game drive at 0630hrs. Return to the hotel at 0800hrs, have breakfast then start your journey back to Nairobi arriving late in the afternoon.

What's Included

Return Transport in Tour Van.
Game Drives in Amboseli National park.
3 Days Accommodation.
All meals for the 3 days plus tea / coffee.

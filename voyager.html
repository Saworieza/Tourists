Voyager Ziwani Package Itinerary

Day 1Nairobi-Voyager Ziwani

Leave Nairobi 7a.m in the morning to arrive Kilaguni for lunch. You may swim or take afternoon siesta before Evening Game drive at 3.30p.m till 5.30p.m. After sunset return to the lodge for dinner and over night.

Day 2Voyager Ziwani

Start with early morning game drive. After breakfast swim and relax till 11a.m proceed to Mzima springs viewpoint where you will have picnic lunch em back for evening game drive. return to the lodge for dinner and overnight.

Day 3Voyager Ziwani-Nairobi

Early morning drive at 6.30am. Breakfast at 9.00 am and checking out at 10am and relax till 12.00pm when you leave via Emali. Lunch at emali at own cost.  Arrive Nairobi at around 5pm

What's Included

Return Transport in Tour Van.
Game Drives in Tsavo West National park.
3 Days Accommodation.
All meals for the 3 days plus tea / coffee.
Services of professional tour guide driver.

What's Excluded

Park entry fee Ksh 1,200 per person per day.
Drinks and tips for waiters, porters and professional tour guides.
Laundry or any other items of personal nature.
